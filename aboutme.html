My life thus far has been a journey of learning and discovery that has shaped my desire for higher education. I was born in Largo, Florida but have lived in other places finally settling in northern Florida. I was exposed to many activities while growing up chief among them sports, Future Farmers of America, and Boy Scouts. Traveling for adventure and fun at theme amusement parks is something that I also enjoy. During college breaks, I volunteer at A Plus Learning Academy, an alternate school for students with disabilities. I enjoyed growing up in a home with both parents and two sisters with many memorable moments, which we continue to make. With the support of friends and family my future will be Simply Amazing!
