Activities

Spotlight on Youth

The Spotlight on Youth event is held annually to highlight the positive creative and artistic accomplishments of young people in North Central Florida. A contest is held to design a T-shirt and Program Cover for the event. In 2011 I was fortunate to win with my design. To see more on this event visit www.cdsfl.org.

Florida Youth Leadership Forum hosted by The Able Trust

The Able Trust a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring fair employment opportunities for Floridians with disabilities hosts the Florida Youth Leadership Forum annually. YLF is designed to teach young people about personal leadership and career planning through workshops, social activities, and mentoring opportunities. I have participated in YLF since 2010 to present advancing from a delegate, support staff member, and currently an adult staff member helping with coordination of the event. For more information on, The Able Trust visit www.abletrust.org.

Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society

The Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society at Gateway College recognizes excellent academic achievements. A grade point average of 3.5% is required to join the society with continued grade point average of 3.0% required to maintain membership. I was inducted on March 9, 2015. For more information visit www.ptk.org.

Art Club

The Art Club at Florida Gateway College actively participates in community service projects and Student Activities. Art Club members raise funds to host and attend workshops with local artist to learn different techniques. Members also visit other colleges and universities as well as museums throughout the area. I have participated in Art Club since 2013.

Special Olympics

Athletes are the heart of the movement. Athletes not only have the opportunity to develop physical fitness and become competitive athletes, but through sport, they build self-esteem, social skills, friendships, leadership skills and have fun. For more information visit http://specialolympicsflorida.org
