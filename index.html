My name is Travis Ford and I am attending Florida Gateway College with the intention of earning certification in Graphic Design Production and Video Game Design.   I like using my talent to create art using the computer and other media to further my career in graphic design with a focus on animation. 


During my High School years I developed an imaginary character, “Teek”, which I incorporated in short animated videos to give examples on how to prevent bullying in schools.


I hope to fulfill my dream of becoming an animator and graphic artist by graduating from Florida Gateway College.  I plan to continue my education at the University of Central Florida in the future and apply my skills to get a good job.
